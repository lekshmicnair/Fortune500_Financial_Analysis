Welcome to the world of Fortune 500 companies

Want to know more about the companies that made this years Fortune 500 list? These companies represents two-thirds of the US economy, with $14 trillion in revenue.

The goal of the project is to analyze the financials and stock prices for the Fortune 500 companies. We also plan to create interactive visualizations for users to explore the top companies, stock analysis and a map showing location of each of these companies.
